2. What CleanReel does

CleanReel is a browser tool for editing short videos that you own or have the rights to edit. It can remove watermarks, erase unwanted objects, enhance and upscale, reframe to a new aspect ratio, and apply a privacy blur to faces (license-plate detection is in beta).

  • Previews are free — a short preview of the processed clip, so you can check the result before spending anything.
  • Exports are paid — exporting the full clip requires signing in and consumes one prepaid credit per export.
  • Limits apply per clip (currently about 60 seconds and 200 MB per video, with output resolution capped to a server-safe size). We may change these limits as the service evolves.

Processing is automated. Results depend on your footage, and some marks or objects clean up better than others — that's exactly what the free preview is for.
