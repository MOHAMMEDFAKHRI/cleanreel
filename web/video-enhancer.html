FAQ

How is this different from a sharpen filter?

Filters exaggerate existing pixels; neural restoration reconstructs detail — and faces get a dedicated restoration model.

Why does enhance cost 2 credits?

Every frame runs through a GPU neural network — it's by far the heaviest operation we offer. Previews stay free.

Will faces look artificial?

Face restore is tuned conservatively; check the free preview and dial sharpening down if you prefer a softer look.
