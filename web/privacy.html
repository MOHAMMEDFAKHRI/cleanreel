Privacy Policy

Last updated 10 July 2026

This is provided in good faith and isn't legal advice; we recommend a lawyer review for your situation.

CleanReel (cleanreel.app) is operated by GREATER ADELAIDE SALES PTY LTD, an Australian company. The short version: we collect the minimum needed to run the tool — your email and the video you're editing — your videos are deleted from our server within about 6 hours, we never see your card number, and there's no third-party ad tracking on the site.

1. What we collect

  • Your email address — used to send your magic sign-in link and to keep your credit balance attached to your account. Previews don't need an account at all.
  • The videos you upload — processed only to produce the result you asked for (preview or export). We don't use your videos for anything else, including training AI models.
  • Payment confirmation — payments are handled entirely by Stripe. Stripe tells us that you paid and which credit pack you bought; we never see or store your card number.
  • Basic technical logs — like any web service, our servers see IP addresses and request metadata, which we use only for security, debugging and rate limiting.

2. How long we keep it

  • Uploaded files and generated results are automatically deleted from our server within about 6 hours. Download your result promptly — after that window it's gone.
  • Quality review: free preview clips (the short before/after pair our tool renders) may be kept a little longer for internal quality review, so we can see what worked and fix what didn't. Where our automatic quality scorer flags a poor result, the uploaded file may be kept for the same review. These copies are deleted automatically within 72 hours, are only ever seen by the operator, and are never shared or used for anything except improving the tool. Full paid exports are not included in this review. If you'd like a specific upload purged immediately, email support@cleanreel.app.
  • Your email address and credit balance are kept while you have an account, so your credits are there when you come back.
  • Stripe retains payment records according to its own policies (financial-record laws require some retention).

3. Who processes it for us (sub-processors)

ProviderWhat it does with your data
RenderHosts the API and does the video processing (your uploads live here, briefly).
NeonDatabase storing your email and credit balance.
StripePayment processing (card details go to Stripe, not to us).
ResendSends the magic-link sign-in emails.
NetlifyServes the website itself (static pages / CDN).

These providers act on our instructions. Some are located overseas (mainly the United States), so your information may be stored or processed outside Australia.

4. In your browser

The only thing CleanReel keeps in your browser is a signed session token (localStorage key cr_auth) so you stay signed in on that device. Signing out removes it. We don't run third-party advertising trackers or analytics cookies.

5. What we never do

  • We don't sell your personal information.
  • We don't use your videos for anything except producing your result.
  • We don't send marketing email — the only emails you get are the sign-in links you request.

6. Your rights

Email support@cleanreel.app from your sign-in address to access or delete your data — we'll show you what we hold (your email and credit balance; uploads are already auto-deleted) or delete your account record. We handle personal information in line with Australian privacy law, and if you're unhappy with our response you can complain to the OAIC (oaic.gov.au).

7. Changes and contact

If we change how we handle your data, we'll update this page and its date. Questions: support@cleanreel.app · misuse reports: abuse@cleanreel.app.
