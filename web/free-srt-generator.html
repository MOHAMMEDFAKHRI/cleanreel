FAQ

What's the catch — why is it free?

The transcript is free because many people then want the burned-in version, which is the paid export. If you only need the .srt, it's yours.

Does it handle accents and background noise?

The AI speech model is robust to accents; heavy background noise reduces accuracy — the free preview shows you exactly what you'll get.

What's an SRT file exactly?

A plain-text subtitle standard: numbered lines with start/end timestamps. Nearly every video platform and player accepts it.
