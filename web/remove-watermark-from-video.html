FAQ

Can I remove any watermark?

No — CleanReel is only for content you own or are licensed to edit, like your own exports or an old logo of yours. Removing someone else's watermark from their content may infringe their rights.

Will it leave a blur or smudge?

No. Instead of blurring, neural inpainting reconstructs the background behind the mark, and a quality score tells you how clean the result is.

Does it work on moving watermarks?

Marked regions can be tracked across frames. Semi-transparent and opaque marks use different removal strategies automatically.
