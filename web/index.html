FAQ

How do I remove a watermark from a video?

Upload a short video you own, mark the watermark (or let CleanReel auto-detect it), preview free, then export the cleaned video.

Is it free?

Previews are free. Full exports use low-cost credits that cover GPU processing — 1 credit per export, except Enhance which uses 2 (it runs every frame through a neural upscaler on a GPU).

Will it blur my video or ruin faces?

No — faces and detailed areas are protected and your audio is preserved, so the subject stays sharp.

What can I legally remove?

Only content you own or are licensed to edit — e.g. your own free-tier exports, your old logo, or stray text/timecode.

What else can it do besides watermarks?

Erase any unwanted object (brush it once — tracking follows it if it moves), enhance & upscale soft or noisy clips, auto-reframe landscape video to 9:16, 1:1 or 4:5 with the subject kept in frame, and privacy-blur faces or license plates (auto-detected and tracked) plus any region you brush.

CleanReel is for content you own or are licensed to edit. Uploads are processed only for your request and auto-deleted from our servers within about 6 hours.
